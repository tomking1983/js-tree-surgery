ABOUT US

Fully Qualified & Insured

J's Tree Surgery Services are a family run business based in Llanfyllin, Powys.

We pride ourselves on offering a friendly and professional service and are fully equipped to undertake any type of tree work that you require.

We carry out tree felling, maintenance and surgery for both domestic and commercial customers throughout most areas of Powys and Shropshire

Please get in touch for a free quote.

Our Services

Tree Felling

J's Tree Surgery are specialists in tree removal and tree felling services; being able to remove any species and size of tree safely, efficiently and under difficult and demanding surroundings.

Tree Pruning

We can provide a full tree pruning and tree maintenance service to our customers throughout most of Shropshire and Powys.

Hedge Cutting

We are experts at providing hedge cutting and hedge maintenance services for both domestic and commercial customers. We have the equipment and knowledge to take care of any type of hedge.

Ivy Removal

Large areas of ivy climbing the walls can cause damp inside your home as it holds moisture against the brickwork as well as causing a lot of structural damage. We are able to remove any unwanted ivy and dispose of it off-site.

Chipping & Log Cutting

Any brash that has been cut can be chipped on or off site. If you have any wood that you would like to use as logs then we can cut them up for you so they are ready for storage.

Crown Reduction & Lifting

Crown reductions and lifts can be completed on trees of any size. We are experienced and skilled at performing crown reductions and lifts safely and effectively.
